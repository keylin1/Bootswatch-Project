Montréal

Montreal, a vibrant metropolis nestled along the
St. Lawrence River, entices with its harmonious
blend of Old World charm, modern sophistication,
and rich cultural tapestry.

December 28, 2023 - January 4, 2024

SFO - YUL


My family and I journeyed to Montréal to reunite with our extended relatives and explore the city's vibrant culture. Amidst our adventures, we delighted in the enchanting sight of snow blanketing the cityscape, adding a magical touch to our memorable trip.

We also indulged in various family bonding activities such as volleyball matches, crafting gingerbread houses, intense games of Werewolf, and even sharing the exhilarating experience of learning how to snowboard together at Ski Mont-Saint-Bruno.
